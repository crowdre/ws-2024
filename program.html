The 8th International Workshop on Crowd-Based Requirements Engineering (CrowdRE'24)



24 June 2024
Held in Reykjavik, Iceland
In conjunction with RE 2024

Program

CrowdRE 2024 is a half-day workshop held on June 24th, 2024. CrowdRE'24 is co-located with the RE 2024 conference. The workshop intends to promote lively discussions among participants from both industry and academia.

The paper session in the below program includes 25-minute slots (15-minute presentation, and 10-minute discussion) for each paper. The presenter of each paper is underlined.

13:45–15:15 Keynote Session (session chair: Julian Frattini)
Welcome by the organizers
Keynote: "Requirements Engineering and Machine Learning: Intentionality and the Crowd"
Jennifer Horkoff
15:15–15:45 Coffee break
15:45–17:30 Paper session (session chair: Oliver Karras)
"Deciphering Empathy in Developer Responses: A Hybrid Approach Utilizing the Perception Action Model and Automated Classification"
Kezia Devathasan, Nowshin Nawar Arony, Kiev Gama and Daniela Damian. Discussant: Sabrina Hoelzer.
"From Isolation to Inclusion: Innovating Digital Health Solutions for Rare Diseases - The Potential of Crowd-based Requirement Engineering"
Sabrina Hoelzer. Discussant: Farnaz Fotrousi.
"Requirements Conflicts Detection: Advancing with Conversational AI"
George Kisso and Farnaz Fotrousi. Discussant: Kiev Gama.
Closing
